ABOUT US

We Are CandiGo

CandiGo is redefining the video interview experience. Our mission is to create a seamless, pressure-free process for the "candidate."

We do this by pioneering the "per-question upload" model. No more long, error-prone, single-take videos. With CandiGo, you record your answer to one question, press "next", and that video uploads immediately while you move on.

It's simple, efficient, and designed to help you go to the next step in your career.
